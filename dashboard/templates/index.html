NYC HOUSING PRICES

How unemployment and other factors affect housing prices in NYC

Hypothesis

How unemployment alter the housing prices in NYC. Our working hypothesis is that with the rising unemployment rate housing prices would go down and vice versa. Our analysis will show how unemployment affects prices in different zip codes and boroughs and if income has any impact on housing prices. We will also discuss other factors affecting the housing price, which one has the biggest impact and how they affect the price.
